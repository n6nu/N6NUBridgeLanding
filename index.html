N6NU SDR Bridges

Windows installers for the SDR bridges that feed wideband I/Q into a modified WSJT-X / QMAP for wide-band Q65 EME and other digital modes. Pick the bridge for your hardware.

Each release is a drop-in upgrade from the previous one. Latest family release: v1.1.5. See the beta-tester guide PDF for setup, troubleshooting, and the technical reference.
